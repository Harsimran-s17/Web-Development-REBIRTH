GrandMa's Apple Pie Recipe

This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generations and never fails to delight

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup jaggery
  • 2 tsp grounded cinnamon
  • 6 medium sized apples, peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F(190°C)
  2. In a bowl, mix sugar and cinnamon
  3. Toss the apples in sugar-cinnamon mixture
